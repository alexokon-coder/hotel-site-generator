The History

Arcadia's only bed & breakfast

About the Home:

Step into the heart of Arcadia, where our exclusive bed and breakfast is housed within the walls of a 101-year-old home. This isn't just a structure; it's a living archive of the town's history. As locals, we take pride in preserving the unique charm that each antique furnishing and creaky floorboard holds. The home stands as a testament to Arcadia's rich cultural legacy, inviting you to embark on a journey through time within its storied walls.

About the Bed and Breakfast:

Our commitment to reopening this cherished haven goes beyond business—it's a labor of love. We want to offer more than just accommodation; we aim to immerse you in the living history of our community. The decision to revive this bed and breakfast is rooted in a deep sense of community pride and a desire to share the cultural legacy embedded in every detail. As you cross the threshold, envision a warm embrace of hospitality that transcends time, where the past meets the present, and every stay adds a chapter to the ongoing story of Arcadia's exclusive bed and breakfast. Welcome to a place where the echoes of history resonate, and where each guest becomes a cherished part of our enduring tale.
